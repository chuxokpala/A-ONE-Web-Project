Welcome to AESL

AESL is an innovation-driven company. With our expert team and best practices, we deliver solutions in the energy and electricity industry in a sustainable way, without compromising the livelihood of future generations. We especially focus on solar energy systems (of all sizes and purposes), and with our team of engineers and powerful collaborations, we deliver projects and services across other market segments.

We welcome NGOs, private individuals, and government bodies wishing to execute sustainable initiatives in renewable energy and our other market segments.
