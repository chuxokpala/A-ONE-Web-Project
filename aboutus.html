About Us

Our Story

A-ONE Electric & Solar Limited (AESL) is an indigenous renewable energy company duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria RC 1419034 to develop renewable energy power projects and offer services in the Oil & Gas industry with our office located in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria.

We believe in strong partnerships with you our clients to create integrated solutions and reach classical solutions. You'll get the benefits of a team that listens. A team that is trained, experienced, exposed globally, creative and dedicated to service. A team that ensures that key deliverables are met and surpassed.

We believe in our name A-ONE and always strive to live up to first-class expectations and nothing less in our solutions and projects. We know how important one project is both to us and our clients in order to keep a healthy business and business relationship going and so we work with this in mind, to keep our clients consistently satisfied.

Our Mission

To deliver sustainable energy solutions that empower businesses and communities with reliable, cost-effective, and environmentally responsible access to energy.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative and sustainable energy solutions in Africa.
